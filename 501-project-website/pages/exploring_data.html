Exploring Data

Before moving on to perform machine learning algorithms on the cleaned dataset to answer the data science questions, some of them can be studied first by using visualising methods and statistical summaries etc., and EDA will also provide some insights to the datasets.



Students Enrollment 2015-2020 Dataset

Looking back at the topic of this project and the first question, now is a good time to find out about the difference between boys and girls in terms of enrollments using this dataset. First, we will look at the histograms about the enrollments situation of girls and boys.

From the plots, the enrollments of girls and boys have similar distribution over 0 and 5000, and also have similar means. But to find out the real case, a hypothesis test will be used:
H0: the average enrollments of girls are equal with boys
H1: the average enrollments of girls are less than boys

The result from the hypothesis test shows the p-value to be 0.029 which is smaller than 0.05, so at 5% significance level we can reject the null hypothesis and conclude that the average enrollments of girls in schools of grades 1 to 12 are less than boys.
